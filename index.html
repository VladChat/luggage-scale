uPatch Luggage Scale — User Manual

Product Overview

Battery-free digital luggage scale for home and travel. Shake to activate; no batteries required. Max capacity 50 kg / 110 lb.

Quick Start (Shake to Activate)

  • Hold the scale and shake firmly several times to power on. Feeling the internal mechanism bounce is normal operation.
  • Wait for 0.0 on the display.
  • Press UNIT to choose kg or lb.

How to Weigh (Strap Hook)

Using the Strap

  • Thread the strap through the luggage handle and clip it back to the scale.
  • Lift the bag by the scale body. Keep the scale vertical and steady.
  • When the reading stabilizes, note the value and lower the bag.

Tip: Lift slowly, keep the scale vertical, and let the number settle before reading.
